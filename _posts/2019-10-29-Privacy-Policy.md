### 1.2 Platform

### 1.3 My games 
All products the developer released on the platforms and currently on sale.

### 1.4 "You" and "User"
Entity who has purchased and played my games.

### 1.5 Saved files
Data saved in local user's device which discribes the progress of games.

## 2 Declaration
1. My games do not collect any user information.
2. The developer does not share user information with any third-party entities.
3. Saved files do not imply any user information, and cannot be conveyed to any other apps or any other entities out of the user's device.
4. You can delete your saved files.
5. My games read the device id, graphic type, size of screen, language of your device, the information mentioned above is the mere things about your device that my games have access to.
6. For iOS device, given that Game Center is successfully authenticated, my games process your saved files and send them to Game Center. To mention twice, the saved files and the way they are processed do not imply any user information.

## 3 Spirit and Principles
The developer respects your rights for your privacy, and aims at protecting your privacy, and treasures the mutual trust between developer and users.
