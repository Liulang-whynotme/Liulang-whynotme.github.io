The drafting, design, debugging, releasing and such work is primarily done by the developer. If you want to know about the art direct, music producer and such people, you can read "Credit" in my games.  

The fonts in my games are embeded fonts and they are license free. They can be displayed normally and are indepentent of your device type.  

My games support two types of graphics: Metal and OpenGLES2. If your device runs OpenGLES2, the quality for reflection of objects is slightly weaker than Metal. If OpenGLES2, some of my games lose a clip of animation on their booting up. These are the only differences in users' experiences than those using Metal.
